▻ I am pleased to announce that I have started a postdoctoral position at the University of Washington. I am co-leading a review of a number of policies to protect salmon habitat and preserve forests on small forest landowner properties in the state of Washington. The project will make use of plot-level biophysical data, forest owner surveys, and causal inference techniques (among others) to present a report to the Washington state legislature.

▻ I was a visiting researcher at the US Family Forest Research Center (FFRC) for the fall 2019 and early 2020. The purpose of my visit was to work with Dr. Brett Butler and the team at the FFRC on analysis of the United States National Woodland Owner Survey.
